The Light Roast

Light roast coffee beans offer a bright, tangy flavor with fruity and floral notes, preserving the unique characteristics of the bean's origin, with higher caffeine content and more antioxidants than darker roasts.

The Medium Roast

Medium roast coffee beans provide a balanced flavor with moderate acidity, a fuller body, and notes of caramel, chocolate, and nuts, while maintaining a harmonious blend of the beans' origin characteristics and roast profile.

The Dark Roast

Dark roast coffee beans feature a bold, rich flavor with low acidity and deep notes of chocolate, caramel, and toasted nuts, often accompanied by a smoky or bittersweet finish, emphasizing the roast's intensity over the bean's original characteristics.
